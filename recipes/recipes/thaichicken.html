Thai Green Curry with Chicken

Aromatic and spicy curry from Thailand

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (500 g, cubed)
  • Thai green curry paste (2-3 tbsp)
  • Coconut milk (1 can, 400 ml)
  • Chicken stock (250 ml)
  • Eggplant (1 large, cubed)
  • Bamboo shoots (1 can, 400 g)
  • Kaffir lime leaves (4-5)
  • Basil leaves (a handful)
  • Fish sauce (1 tbsp)
  • Brown sugar (1 tbsp)
  • Lime juice (1 tbsp)

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot or wok over medium heat. Add the curry paste and stir for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the cubed chicken and stir until it is coated with the curry paste.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken stock. Add the eggplant, bamboo shoots, kaffir lime leaves, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring the curry to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Stir in the lime juice and basil leaves just before serving. Serve hot with steamed rice.
