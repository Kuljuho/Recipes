Oven-Fetapasta

Creamy and tasteful Oven-Fetapasta is a perfect choice for autumn

Ingredients

  • Feta (200 gr)
  • Pasta (100 gr)
  • Small tomatoes (the more the better)
  • Olive oil
  • Black pepper

    • Steps

    1. Add feta to a glass tray or something similar
    2. Wash the tomatoes and pour them in
    3. Pour much olive oil on feta and on tomatoes
    4. Add black pepper
    5. Oven for a 20 minutes on 200 celsius and then on grill-mode 10 minutes 225 celsius
    6. Cook the pasta
    7. Smash them together
    8. Eat it all or most of it
